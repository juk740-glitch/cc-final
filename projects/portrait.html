filet_crochet.js

a data portrait of the amount of time i spent filet crocheting in a week, represented in a filet crochet pattern. each row represents a day of the week, and each grid represents an hour in the day in military time, starting from 0:00 to 23:00. the filled grids are the number of hours I crocheted, creating a filet crochet piece that represents my filet crochet addiction.
